Guidelines for Batch Guardian

  1. Faculty Batch Guardian will arrange meetings with students once in a month.
  2. Faculty Batch Guardian will assure that monitoring details are filled/updated by the students.
  3. During follow-up meetings, the Batch Guardian will get information about the students' attendance and counsel them for irregularities, if any.
  4. Faculty Batch Guardians have to make the performance analysis of individual students on the basis of their attendance and test marks.
  5. After every internal evaluation, the Batch Guardian will get information about the students' academic performance and understand reasons for poor performance, if observed.
  6. Batch Guardians are expected to encourage students to participate in both institute and external activities to explore their special skills and abilities.
  7. The record of all meetings has to be maintained by all Faculty Batch Guardians individually for every student.
  8. Such records will be used to share with the respective parents during parent meetings and will be produced to the management whenever required.
